Professor Evia

We have gotten the opportunity to work with Adobe softwares, picture editing softwares, and different media platforms such as magazine spreads and websites.


Professor Evia has been at Virginia Tech since 2004. He has been teaching classes at both the graduate level and the undergraduate level.
